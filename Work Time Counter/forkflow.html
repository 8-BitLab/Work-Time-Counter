Fork Flow APP — Open Source Team Productivity Suite

Fork Flow

Free Team Time Tracking & Collaboration Platform

Stop paying for Jira, Clockify, and Monday.com. Fork Flow gives your team time tracking, real-time chat, task boards, file sharing, and reporting — completely free, forever. Open source. No subscriptions. No limits.

✅ Active & Ready to Use

Key Features

Everything your startup needs to stay productive — built into one lightweight desktop app.

⏱ Time Tracking

Start/stop timer with project categories. Auto-stop safety after 10 hours. Real-time sync across the team via Firebase.

👥 Team Status

See who is Online, Working, or Offline in real time. Heartbeat updates every 60 seconds. Sound alerts for status changes.

💬 Team Chat

Built-in group chat with sound notifications. No Slack needed. Messages sync in real time through Firebase.

📋 Sticker Board

Kanban-style task board with priorities (High/Medium/Low), types (Todo/Bug/Idea/Reminder), and assignment to team members.

📁 File Sharing

Drag-and-drop file sharing up to 5 MB. Password-protected downloads. No third-party storage needed.

📖 Wiki / Knowledge Base

Shared team knowledge base with categories. Admins can add, edit, and organise. Color-coded notes for quick reference.

🍅 Pomodoro Timer

Built-in 25/5/15 Pomodoro technique timer with visual progress ring. Works independently alongside time tracking.

📊 Reports & Export

PDF monthly reports, CSV export, printable summaries. Breakdown by project, by date, with total hours calculated automatically.

🚀 Jira Integration

Connect to Jira Cloud and pull your assigned issues directly into Fork Flow. View, filter, and track Jira tasks without leaving the app.

📝 Standup Generator

Auto-generate daily standup summaries from your tracked work. Yesterday's tasks, today's plan — ready to paste into any meeting or chat.

The Problem

Small companies and startups face real challenges every single day.

⚠️ What Small Teams Struggle With

  • Team members scattered across different time zones — nobody knows who is working when
  • Missed tasks and bugs fall through the cracks without a shared board
  • Miscommunication between developers, testers, and managers
  • No visibility into who is doing what, how long tasks take, or where time goes
  • Expensive tools like Jira ($8-16/user/month), Monday.com ($10/user/month), Clockify Pro ($12/user/month) — costs add up fast
  • Tool fatigue — switching between Jira, Slack, Google Drive, Clockify, and Notion just to manage a 5-person team
  • No budget for enterprise software when you're a startup trying to survive

✅ How Fork Flow Fixes Everything

  • Real-time team status — instantly see who is Online, Working, or Offline across all time zones
  • Shared task board with priorities and assignments — no more lost bugs or forgotten tasks
  • Built-in team chat — communicate instantly without switching apps
  • Live time tracking — everyone sees what everyone is working on, with automatic duration logging
  • Completely free — $0 per user, $0 per month, $0 forever. MIT license.
  • All-in-one — time tracking + chat + task board + file sharing + wiki + reports in a single app
  • Your data, your server — Firebase backend you own and control. No vendor lock-in.

How It Works

Get your team up and running in under 10 minutes.

1
Download & Extract — Grab the latest release from GitHub. Unzip to any folder. No installer needed.
2
Set Up Firebase — Create a free Firebase Realtime Database. Paste your URL into the app config. Done.
3
Add Your Team — Edit the user list in UserStorage.cs. Build once. Share the exe with your team.
4
Start Working — Select your name, log in, and press START. Your team can see you working in real time.
5
Collaborate — Chat with your team, share files, add tasks to the board, ping teammates, and track every hour.
6
Generate Reports — Press Ctrl+P for a monthly PDF report. Ctrl+E for CSV export. Share with your clients or investors.
